Structural Modifications Applied
  • Simplification (T29, T10, T3): Structure reduced to bring costs within valid ceiling/floor bounds before reassignment to 8k–9k and 3k–4k.
  • Complication (T20, T22, T17): Added structural complexity to deliberately drive optimizer plan costs upward into higher cost bins.
  • Quota Expansion (T3, T4, T8, T19): Instance generation counts increased to fill the critically under-populated 3k–4k bin to its ground-truth target of 27.
  • 0k–1k Throttling (T2, T6, T9, T16): Four templates had volumes cut to drain the overcrowded low-end bin — combined with T17's redirect, this brought 0k–1k from 36 down to the GT target of 20.
  • 5k–6k Throttling (T7, T11, T13, T15): Four templates throttled to decompress the most congested bin; T22 was simultaneously complicated to migrate its 4 queries to 6k–7k, doubling as congestion relief.
  • Purging (T1, T32): Completely removed from the pipeline — their presence created synthetic noise in a bin with zero ground-truth observations.
Post-Refinement Outcome Summary
  • OOB Rate: 9.1% at profiling (T3, T10, T29 all invalid) → 0% OOB post-refinement. All three fully rescued.
  • 2k–3k Void: Completely eliminated — T17's migration fills the entire deficit with 9 correctly placed queries.
  • 0k–1k Noise: Dramatically reduced from 36 initial profiling queries to the ground-truth target of 20 through throttling of T2, T6, T9, T16 plus T17 redirect out of the bin.
  • 5k–6k Congestion: Resolved by throttling T7, T11, T13, T15 and migrating T22's 4 queries to 6k–7k — bringing the bin from 42 profiling queries down to the GT target of 29.
  • 3k–4k Peak: Brought from 11 at profiling to exactly 27 — a perfect match with ground truth, via T3 rescue plus T4, T8, T19 expansion.
  • High-cost tail (8k–10k): Coverage restored from 5 profiling queries to 20 precisely placed queries matching ground truth targets.
  • 4k–5k pollution: Cleaned to zero — T1 and T32 removed, preserving the semantically meaningful gap from the original workload.
